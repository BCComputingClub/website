More Than a Computer Club!

We are a campus organization with affiliation to the Association for Computing Machinery
  • This site is currently under construction
  • Please be patient while we update the website

  • Our purpose is to increase awareness and usage of technology with an emphasis on computing technology
    (hardware, software, operating systems, etc).
  • The BC ACM is made up of students from different majors, so any student can join.
  • Activites/Social Events:
    • Programming (yes, this counts as social!)
    • Pizza Parties/Game Night/Movie Night
    • Professional guest speakers sponsored by ACM
    • Preparing for the ACM programming competition in October/November
    • Afternoon programming help sessions
  • Each year three teams from Bridgewater go to the ACM competition in October/November.
