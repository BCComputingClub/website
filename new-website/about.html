The Bridgewater College Computing Club

What We Do

Throughout the year, we meet on a weekly basis to discuss computing technologies. Often, one of our members will give a "lightning talk"—a short discussion about a specific computing-related subject—or we may plan for an event, such as attending the ACM ICPC or our annual video game tournament fundraiser.

3D Printing

The BC Computing Club serves as the parent organization for the 3D Printing Club. This means that all members of the 3D Printing club are members of the BC Computing Club and vice versa. The 3D Printing Club has a separate meeting time from the BC Computing Club, but 3D Printing members are encouraged to attend BC Computing Club meetings and vice versa.

Becoming a Member

To join the BC Computing Club, just attend our weekly meetings. To find the time and place for the next meeting, please check out our Calendar. If you are already a club member and have signed up for club emails, you should receive an email notification from the President or Vice President before the meeting.
